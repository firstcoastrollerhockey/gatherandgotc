Insider Tips

Plan Smarter, Wait Less

⚡

Express Pass = Game Changer

Premier resort guests get Unlimited Express Pass included — bypassing standby lines on most rides. This can save hours every day.

🦉

Start at Islands of Adventure

Hagrid's Motorbike Adventure is the #1 most popular ride. Head there first thing at rope drop before the lines explode.

🔗

Pair with Disney

Universal and Disney are only 15 minutes apart. Many families do 4–5 days Disney + 2–3 days Universal for the ultimate Orlando trip.

🍺

Try the Butterbeer

It's a rite of passage. Cold, frozen, or hot — every member of your family needs to try it in Hogsmeade or Diagon Alley.
